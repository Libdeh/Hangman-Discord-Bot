The Pigeon: A Discord Bot

How to Play:

  • To start a new game, send $play in messages.
  • A random word from a dictionary of 10,000 words will be chosen and sent as blanks by the bot.
  • To guess a single letter, use the $p command.
  • To guess the full word, use the !p command.
  • 6 chances to guess the word or you lose the game!
